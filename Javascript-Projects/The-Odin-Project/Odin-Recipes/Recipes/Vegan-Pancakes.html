My Mother's Own Vegan Pancakes

This reminds me of my childhood in the hills of Mongolia and the planes of Switzerland. I always had these vegan pancakes doused in gravy and hotsauce. Really reminds me of the time before cell phones, back when we all wrote with quills.

Recipe

  1. In a medium bowl, add the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt, and stir to combine.
  2. In a medium bowl or liquid measuring cup, add almond milk, apple cider vinegar, and vanilla, and stir to combine
  3. Pour the liquid mixture into the dry mixture and whisk until smooth.
  4. Let batter rest for 5 minutes.
  5. Pour about Â½ cup (65 grams) of batter onto a nonstick pan or griddle over medium heat.
  6. When the top begins to bubble, flip the pancake and cook until golden.
  7. Serve warm with maple syrup, gravy, and hotsauce.
